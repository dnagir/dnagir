Billiard Is Interesting

Nov 21st, 2006

I sometimes play billiard to enjoy and have some relax. I took a cue in my hands tens times and thought I can hit balls very well, but when I played with a girls who’s specialict in billiard I lost four games with zero result. Well, it was really nice to watch her play. And I also won that day one of my friends first time! Not sure how… But now I want to have some more games and train myself. If my patience will allow that… Russian billiard is very interesting. There’re a large table, small packets and large balls.
