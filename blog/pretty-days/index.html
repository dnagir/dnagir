Pretty Days

Dec 29th, 2007

I have just heard Pretty Days by Ogden Nash and like the words. People sometimes just need to be nowhere and think about nothing. Or maybe something infinite… Anyway read these lines and do your best to enjoy the New Year.
How pleasant to sit on the beach, On the beach, on the sand, in the sun, With ocean galore within reach, And nothing at all to be done! No letters to answer, No bills to be burned, No work to be shirked, No cash to be earned, It is pleasant to sit on the beach With nothing at all to be done!
